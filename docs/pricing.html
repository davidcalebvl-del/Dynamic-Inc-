Can I try Davora AI before paying?

Yes! Our Free plan gives you full access to basic AI features with 20-30 messages per day. No credit card required.

How do I upgrade my plan?

You can upgrade at any time from your account settings. The upgrade takes effect immediately, and you get a prorated credit for any unused time.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Yes! We offer custom plans for teams and enterprises. Contact our sales team for more information and custom pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you choose annual billing for Pro and Premium plans.
